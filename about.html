Hi, I'm Ellen.

I'm a scientist turned writer, editor, and creative strategist.

As far back as I can remember, I've been a joyful dabbler — the kind of kid who could ace a math test in the morning, hit every note at a piano recital after lunch, and win the soccer game before dinner. I didn't just pick one lane; I learned to run the whole track.

That spirit stuck with me. Today, I'm still that curious, quick-on-her-feet multitasker who channels her energy and skills into writing sharp copy, editing with precision, designing intuitive experiences, and wearing every hat in between.

I have a BA in Economics and Environmental Studies from Tulane University and a Master of Science in Geography from Texas A&M University, where I worked aboard two research cruises studying deep-sea coral in the Pacific and spelunked in a central Texas cave in pursuit of climate knowledge.

I believe that good design and clear narratives can make even the complex feel accessible. I am eager to bring my editorial leadership, scientific rigor, UX sensibility, and love of narrative to your next project.
